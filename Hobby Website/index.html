Hi, my name is Sheng Cruz and what makes me happy? Helping my mom at the garden and the farmers market.

I can't really say I had a choice in picking this hobby. Since I was a old enough to walk, I'm sure my parents brought me to the garden with them. My earliest memories are playing in the garden while my parents worked in the fields picking endless bushels of cucumbers. What I love about the garden/farmers market:

  1. Always having fresh vegetables/fruit
  2. Spending time outside
  3. Enjoying nature
  4. Getting a tan
  5. Making extra money

Our Location

What started out as a hobby in the garden ended up being over 10 years of business at various farmers markets in the Milwaukee area. In the past we have been vendors at:

  • Menomonee Falls Farmers Market
  • Fondy Farmers Market
  • Cathedral Square Market
  • Milwaukee Public Market
